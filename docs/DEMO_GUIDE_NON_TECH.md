# 🎬 SplitRails: Simple Video & Presentation Guide (Non-Technical)

Welcome! This guide is written specifically for presenters, video creators, or judges who are **non-technical**. It explains how to run the SplitRails application on your screen, what to click, and what to say in plain English to wow your audience.

---

## 💡 The Core Message: What is SplitRails?

> **"Three freelancers in three countries share one client's bills. Today, one person fronts the whole cost and spends weeks chasing the others over slow, expensive bank wires."**
>
> **SplitRails replaces that:** One person creates a split invoice, each partner approves their own portion, and the money is held safely by a smart contract on the blockchain until **everyone** has paid. Once complete, it is released to the vendor all at once. If someone doesn't pay, everyone gets an automatic, instant refund.

---

## 🏃‍♂️ Step-by-Step Screen Walkthrough

Here is exactly what to do on screen, along with a simple script you can read or adapt.

### **Pre-requisites before you start recording:**
1. Make sure you are using a desktop browser with the **Freighter Extension** installed.
2. Ensure Freighter is set to **Testnet** and unlocked.
3. Make sure you have at least **15 USDC** in your wallet balance to pay your share during the demo.

---

### 📍 Step 1: The Login Screen
* **What to do:** Open the app and show the Login page. 
* **What to say:**  
  > *"Welcome to SplitRails. We are looking at a modern, premium workspace designed for global teams to handle group expenses without the friction of traditional banks."*
* **Action:** Click **Log In** to go to the main dashboard.

---

### 📍 Step 2: Creating a New Split Invoice
* **What to do:** On the dashboard, click **New Split** in the sidebar or header.
* **Configure these exact values:**
  * **What is this for?** `AWS Infrastructure & API Billing`
  * **Category:** `Software & infra`
  * **Total invoice amount:** `$50.00`
  * **Set due date:** Select any date in the future.
* **Adjust the Splits:**
  * Choose **Percent** as the splitting method.
  * Enter **`25%`** for each of the four participants (making your share exactly **`$12.50`**).
* **What to say:**  
  > *"Creating a shared invoice is simple. Let's create an expense for our group's AWS bill totaling $50.00. We'll split it equally among our 4 team members. SplitRails automatically calculates each person's share to the penny: exactly $12.50 per person."*

---

### 📍 Step 3: Deploying the Smart Contract
* **What to do:** Click **Review split** at the bottom right. On the review page, click **Deploy Escrow & Send**. A Freighter wallet popup will appear. Click **Approve**.
* **What to say:**  
  > *"When we click 'Deploy Escrow', SplitRails does something unique: instead of putting everyone's money in one giant pool, it creates a personal, secure 'digital vault' on the Stellar network specifically for this invoice. This ensures maximum security and transparency."*

---

### 📍 Step 4: Sharing & Paying Your Slice
* **What to do:** On the success screen, click **Copy Link** next to the invite link. Open a new browser tab, paste the link, and hit Enter.
* **What to say:**  
  > *"Now the split is live on-chain. We copy the share link and send it to our team. Let's open the link to see what our teammate experiences when they pay."*

---

### 📍 Step 5: Face ID / Touch ID Approval & Settlement
* **What to do:** Connect your wallet, then click **Approve & Settle**. 
  * Your browser's Face ID / Touch ID prompt will pop up. Tap your sensor.
  * The Freighter wallet popup will open. Click **Approve**.
* **What to say:**  
  > *"When a teammate opens their link, they see their exact share: $12.50. They click 'Approve & Settle'. First, a biometric prompt appears—letting them approve securely using Face ID or Touch ID. Then, they sign the transaction with their wallet. The $12.50 is now locked securely in the digital vault on-chain."*

---

### 📍 Step 6: Locked Success & Receipt
* **What to do:** The screen will transition to the success page displaying the secure lock animation and receipt.
* **What to say:**  
  > *"Perfect. The payment is secured. The funds are held safely by the contract. Once our other teammates pay their shares, the full $50.00 is automatically sent to AWS. If they don't, we can claim a full refund instantly."*

---

### 📍 Step 7: Audit Ledger & Corporate Export
* **What to do:** Go back to the dashboard, click **Audit Ledger** in the sidebar, and click **Export Ledger** to download a CSV.
* **What to say:**  
  > *"Finally, we look at the Audit Ledger. SplitRails automatically builds a live cost timeline and allows finance teams to export structured, tax-compliant records directly to QuickBooks or Xero. This gives businesses a complete, tamper-proof paper trail of their cross-border expenses."*

## 🎨 Design & Visual Features to Highlight

If the viewer asks about the design, or if you want to highlight the look and feel, point out:
* **Glassmorphic Cards:** Notice the frosted-glass effect on the cards, which gives a clean, premium, state-of-the-art software feel.
* **Vibrant Gradients:** We use deep blue, indigo, and orange accents instead of boring flat colors to make the interface feel alive and premium.
* **Micro-Animations:** Pay attention to how the charts load and how buttons subtly shrink when clicked. These animations make the app feel responsive.
